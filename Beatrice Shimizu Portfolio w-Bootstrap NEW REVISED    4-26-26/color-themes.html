Color Themes

Colors influence perception and behavior. A clear color theme makes a site feel polished and professional while reinforcing brand identity. Color Themes bring harmony, clarity, and personality to a website — turning layouts into cohesive visual experiences and guiding the eyes by highlighting important elements (like links or CTA buttons), and also helping to organize content.

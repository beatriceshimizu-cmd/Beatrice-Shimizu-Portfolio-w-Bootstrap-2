Photo Restoration

Photo restoration with Photoshop is the process of repairing damaged, faded, or aged photographs to return them to their original condition. It involves removing tears, scratches, stains, and creases, as well as fixing color fading and enhancing contrast. The goal is to preserve the historical and sentimental value of the photograph while improving its visual quality.
